# NVO987 – Culture Visuelle Moderne et Contemporaine

NVO987 is a French cultural association dedicated to modern and contemporary visual culture. The organization develops interdisciplinary research at the intersection of art history, visual studies, media theory, digital humanities, and artificial intelligence.

Our activities include:

- Research and publications
- Digital humanities projects
- Cultural heritage documentation
- Visual theory and color studies
- AI and computational aesthetics
- Open-source software and knowledge repositories

## Research Areas

- Modern and contemporary visual culture
- Art history
- Media archaeology
- Visual perception
- Algorithmic image cultures
- Artificial intelligence
- Digital archives and semantic web
